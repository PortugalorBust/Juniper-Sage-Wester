Tales Made Along the Way

Breathe, ground yourself. I heard Moms hushed voice in my head as I began meditating. Breathe in, count to four. Breathe out, count to four. Hold. Repeat.. I suddenly felt as though I was floating. I opened my eyes and glanced toward the window to see a hazy, green light from behind the curtain. I gulped my fear down and made my way for the door. I slowly turned the handle and peeked into the eerie hospital hallway. There were hushed voices surrounding the room, I quietly closed the door and inched closer. I saw the girl on the hospital bed again. She was sobbing, not as joyous as youd expect after having a baby. Across the room, I could see another, older woman rocking the newborn wrapped up in a pink blanket in her arms, singing German lullabies.



I'm Jerry, the only teenage daughter of two cycle breaking hippies. I live on the road in a camper with my Mom, a traveling spiritual lifecoach, and my dad, a retired firefighter and Mom's chauffeur. The year I turn sixteen will prove to be the most horrifying and enlightening year of my life. In these pages is the story that is building me, and I hope, on your journey you find the pieces you need to build yourself as well.

About

Juniper Wester lives in Northern Utah, homeschools her daughter, breaks cycles, helps people organize their lives, trains her dogs, cleanses negative energy, raises goats & chickens, grows vegetables, owns a business, loves her husband, and is learning how to give herself more grace.

connect with Me

Jerry, a fifteen year old witch, travels the country with her parents in an RV. Right as she turns sixteen, secrets long buried in her past come to light. As she unravels the truth behind who she is, her entire world and everything she has known burns to the ground. Follow her journey as she comes of age and discovers that some will go through extreme lengths to keep skeletons in the closet.
